# Decora Requirements Specification

Version: 1.0

Status: Draft

1. Project Description
The problem:
A customer who is painting their home has to visit several stores or websites 
to find paints, tools, and accessories.
Solution:
Decora brings together all finishing and decor products on a single platform, 
offering an easy and secure shopping experience.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. Project Goals
Provide an intuitive shopping experience.
Provide secure authentication and authorization.
Store and manage products, customers, and orders efficiently.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Payment Gateway
